Welcome!

I am PhD candidate at the IVUL group KAUST. I pass my days figuring out ways for computers, robots, or artificial agents to perceive and reason about the world as we humans do. My research interest lies in the intersection of computer vision and machine learning. In particular, I’m intrigued about scalable and efficient video understanding. Check out my publications to get a glimpse of my work.
